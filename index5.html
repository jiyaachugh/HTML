Discuss the role of Machine learning in fraud detection, medical diagnosis and email spam detection.

Fraud Detection:

  • Machine learning is extensively used in fraud detection across various industries, including finance, insurance, and e-commerce.
  • Supervised learning algorithms, such as logistic regression, decision trees, and random forests, are employed to build models that learn from historical fraud data.

    Medical Diagnosis:

  • Machine learning contributes significantly to medical diagnosis and healthcare decision making processes.
  • Supervised learning algorithms are used to train models on labeled medical data, such as patient records, diagnostic images, and laboratory results.
  • These models can assist healthcare professionals in diagnosing diseases, predicting patient outcomes, and recommending personalized treatment plans

Email Spam Detection:

  • Email spam detection is one of the earliest and most widely adopted applications of machine learning in cybersecurity.
  • Supervised learning algorithms, such as Naive Bayes, support vector machines (SVMs), and ensemble methods, are commonly used to classify emails as either spam or legitimate (ham)
